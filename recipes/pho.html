Pho Recipe

Description

This recipe originated from South East Asia. Pho is a rice noodle dish topped with vegatables and various choices of protein. Its broth is beef based and can be made with beef paste, herbs, and spices. You can also use chicken broth in placement of beef based broth. This dish has evolved over the years and have recently gotten more popular in the U.S. Condiments can be added to "flavor" the broth to your taste. This recipe is a basic revision of this dish.

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Beef paste or herbs of your choice
  • Your choice of protein
  • Bean Sprout
  • Green Onions
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
  • Comdiments (Ex. Hot sauce, Hoisin sauce, Black pepper, Soy sauce, etc.)

Steps for making Pho

  1. Cook your protein of choice
  2. Add water to a pot and place on top of stove with the stove on
  3. Add your beef paste/herbs to your water
  4. Repeat step 2 with another pot (DO NOT ADD Beef paste or herbs to this pot)
  5. Cut green onions to thin slices
  6. Quarter your lime
  7. Cut you cilantro into 1 inch lengths
  8. Once water from step 3 is boiling, place noodles with noodle strainer inside pot
  9. Remove noodle strainer and place the noodles into a bowl
  10. Can add condiments now or add broth to your bowl first, do both
  11. Add your cut veggies, along side your cooked protein
  12. EAT!
