Steak Recipe

Description

This recipe shows you how to make a great steak. It cooks the steak to a perfect medium rare. This is a simple dish that you can enjoy with rice. It is a perfect pairing with rice because the rice can absorb the juices from the steak. It is also inexpensive but can easily be upgraded depending on the quality of the steak.

Ingredients

  • Steak (Marbling of steak determine your quality/tenderness)
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Butter
  • White Rice

Steps for Steak

  1. Cook your rice
  2. Season steak with salt, black pepper, and garlic powder
  3. Heat up pan to "Medium"
  4. After pan is hot, place oil into pan
  5. Place steak into pan (Time with timer for 2 - 3 Mins Depending of Thickness of Steak)
  6. After timer goes off, Flip steak and repeat timer
  7. After timer goes off, turn heat up to "High" and cook for 30 secs
  8. After timer, flip side and repeat timer for step 7
  9. Place butter into pan, and lower heat to medium
  10. Baste both sides with butter for 30 secs each
  11. Take your steak out of pan and let it rest for at LEAST 5 min
  12. Cut your steak into slices and place them with a plate of rice
  13. EAT!
