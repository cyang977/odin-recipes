Instant Ramen Recipe

Description

This recipe shows you how to make instant ramen. All it takes is 5 mins, a microwave or hot water, and instant noodles. The best part of this dish is that you can dress it up if you want by adding all sorts of ingredients. It is up to your imagination on how fancy you would want to make this dish.

Ingredients

  • Your choice of instant ramen noodles

Steps for Steak

  1. If want to dress up your noodles make steak to go along with it
  2. Take out your Instant noodles
  3. If it is a "Cup" noodles, pour hot water up to the line, close lid and wait 3 - 5 mins
  4. If you do not have hot water, place the noodles with cold water into the microwave for 3 -5 mins (SKIP THIS STEP IF YOU HAVE HOT WATER)
  5. You noodles will then be done!
  6. EAT!
